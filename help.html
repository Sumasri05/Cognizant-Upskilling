Community Event Portal Help

Welcome to the Community Event Portal Help Page. Here you can find information about registration, events, and support.

How to Register

  • Fill in your name and email
  • Select your preferred event
  • Choose the event date
  • Click the Submit button

Event Participation

Community members can participate for free in selected events. External participants may need to pay registration fees.
